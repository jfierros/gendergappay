Close the Gap

Welcome to our page! We created this website in order to educate the world about issues pertaining to unequal pay between genders in the workforce. Currently, the average woman makes about 78% to 82% of what the average man makes, and it is crucial that this gap is closed as soon as possible. Between 1980 and 2000 the gap significantly lessened, but since then, that progress has stalled, closing by less than a nickel. In order to resolve this problem, individuals around the planet need to be aware of it, and we hope that this website will be the first step to achieving that equality.

Gap In Technology

There is a 28.3% base pay diffrence with computer programmers, where women earn $0.72 for every $1.00 men earn.

63% of the time, men were offered higher salaries than women for the same role at the same company. The report found that companies were offering women between 4% and a whopping 45% less starting pay for the same job. More than half of women in the U.S. tech sector are being paid less than their male counterparts
